Bus Buddy®

Enhancing Fairness & Transparency in QLD Public Transport

🌳 The Trunk: The Core MVP

Bus Buddy® is a real-time fare anomaly detection app for Brisbane commuters. It identifies incorrect fare charges, offers dispute steps, predicts travel costs, and maps hotspots of overcharging using real Go Card data and weather info.

🌿 Branches: Future Ideologies

📡 Real-Time Fare Alerts

Users get instant pop-ups or emails when fare anomalies are detected. Future updates will integrate smartwatch & SMS alerts.

📁 Smart Transaction Uploads

Users can upload Go Card data via image or file. Future goal: auto-recognition of fields using OCR & AI.

🗺️ Interactive Heatmaps

Crowdsourced maps show hotspots of overcharging. Weather overlays will help correlate stormy days with missed touch-offs.

📈 Personalised Fare Forecasts

Uses Prophet & linear models to forecast upcoming weekly transport costs. Future idea: sync forecasts to planners/budgeting apps.

🎮 Gamification & Community

Points & badges for reporting anomalies. Future enhancements include reward systems and student discounts for top contributors.

  • Australia-wide system integration

  • Offline reporting features for tourists
